Healthcare Design Challenge


For this project, I was tasked with creating an interface that could be used by nurses and physicians to document patient information in order to administer better care. My goal was to include elements like current and past health, tobaco and alcohol use, family history, and immunizations.

Sketches & Wireframing

I made a variety of sketches to outline the flow of information and how the pages would be organized. I decided on having a single, long-form page for data entry, with two smaller pages for adding additional details to entries.

  • the data entry page, where the nurse would input information about the patient
  • the "edit information" page, where the nurse or physician could edit in detail existing entries about specific health conditions or family history
  • the data presentation page, which would consolidate all of the newly entered data in a readable way so that the physician could easily reference items.

High-Fidelity Mockups
