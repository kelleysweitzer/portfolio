About Me

I am currently a sophomore at the University of Michigan in the College of LSA! I love finding new ways to solve complex problems in the context of design and programming. I hope to one day harness the intersection of art, technology, and people to make tech more accessible for everyone.

In my free time I love to cook vegan food, make Spotify playlists, play violin, and create art in whatever way I can. I'm also passionate about social justice and equality on campus and in the greater community.
